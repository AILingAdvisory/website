About AI LING ADVISORY

We are an expert advisory firm dedicated to bridging the critical gap between deep technology and complex financial regulation.

Liu, Ming (柳溟)

Founder & Director

Expert-Led, Practitioner-Focused.

AI LING ADVISORY was founded by Liu, Ming (柳溟) on a simple premise: most firms lack the specialist talent to govern AI, and most large consultancies offer theoretical advice that fails in practice.

As a dedicated AI GRC expert, Ming established the firm to provide a new model: a boutique advisory service that combines deep regulatory knowledge with practical, hands-on experience in operationalising AI controls.

Our guidance is built for your reality, not just a slide deck. We help you build frameworks that satisfy regulators, empower your teams, and turn your AI governance from a strategic "blocker" into a competitive "enabler."
